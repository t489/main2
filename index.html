preparing them for leadership  and life. 

How does our troop operate?

Our events are scout-led and parent-supported. Senior Scouts lead by example, demonstrating the Scout Law and following the Scout Oath. 

We  plan twelve exciting activities annually - varying from hikes around the bay to snow-camping in the Sierra Nevadas. Our scouts vote on activities annually.



Our meetings

From 7:00pm-8:30pm
